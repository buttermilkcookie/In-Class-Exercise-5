How the Bigfoot Legend Began

July 30th, 2018

When did the Bigfoot, or "Sasquatch," legend begin? In 1958, journalist Andrew Genzoli of the Humboldt Times highlighted a fun, if dubious, letter from a reader about loggers in northern California who'd discovered mysteriously large footprints. "Maybe we have a relative of the Abominable Snowman of the Himalayas," Genzoli jokingly wrote in his September 21 column alongside the letter.
